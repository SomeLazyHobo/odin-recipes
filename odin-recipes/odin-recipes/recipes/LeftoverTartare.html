Leftover Tartare

So good, even your pet rat's will love it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Berries from anyone's garden
  • Mustard
  • Dead/fresh leaves

Preparation Instructions

  1. Chew meat with teeth or rip apart into small pieces with your fingers.. or whatever sharp object is closest to you
  2. Rip/chew the berries into tiny pieces
  3. Mix it all together
  4. Draw a little sun with the mustard to imitate egg yolk
  5. Crumble the leaves into small pieces and spread them around the tartare
  6. Enjoy!
