Fruit Salad

Found some leftover fruits on your hunting journey? Make the perfect fruit salad that contains all the sweetness and nutritions you crave.

Ingredients

  • Peeled orange skins
  • Peeled banana skins
  • Rotten apple cores (The more rotten, the better!)
  • Mushy grapes (Hard ones are fine too)

Preparation Instructions

  1. Prepare your paper bag. If you have a newspaper, lay it flat on the ground/table.
  2. Toss everything you have into the paperbag. For the latter, throw everything onto the newspaper and fold all the edges up so the newspaper forms a bag.
  3. Shake shake it like a polaroid picture
  4. Enjoy!
