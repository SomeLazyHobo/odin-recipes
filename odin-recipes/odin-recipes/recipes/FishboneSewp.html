Fishbone Sewp!

An easy, delicious and warm dish for the cold days.. or when you're broke

Ingredients

  • Sewage water (normal or ground water is fine too)
  • Leftover fish bone's from the dump or your cat's leftovers
  • Grass (optional)

Preparation Instructions

  1. Throw everything into a pot/container and throw it over a fire
  2. Before the sewp starts boiling, take it off the fire
  3. Enjoy!
